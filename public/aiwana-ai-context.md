Aiwana Business Management Suite includes three separate SaaS applications:

- HRMS
- CRM
- ERP

These products can be used individually, together, on subscription, or customized for an organization.

Aiwana HRMS helps businesses manage employees, attendance, leave, payroll, recruitment, onboarding, performance, documents, reports and employee self-service. Aiwana CRM helps companies manage leads, customers, follow-ups, reminders, pipeline visibility and sales operations.

### Aiwana ERP
